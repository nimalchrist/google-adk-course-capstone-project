# Welcome to ElectroMart eComBot! 🛒

Hi there! I'm your **ElectroMart shopping assistant** powered by AI.

## What I can do

- **📦 Order Support** — Track orders, check delivery status, handle cancellations and returns
- **🛒 Product Recommendations** — Find the perfect device for your needs and budget
- **🔄 Returns & Refunds** — Guide you through our return process
- **❓ FAQs** — Answer questions about policies, shipping, and warranty

## How it works

Behind the scenes, I use a **multi-agent system**:
- An **Orchestrator** decides who should handle your question
- A **Support Agent** handles order-related queries
- A **Sales Agent** handles product recommendations

You'll see **steps** and **cards** in the UI showing what's happening internally!

## Quick start

Try asking:
- "Where is my order ORD-001?"
- "Recommend a phone under ₹80,000"
- "Compare iPhone 15 Pro vs Samsung Galaxy S24"
- "What's your return policy?"
